## 🚀 Selected Projects

### 💰 MSquare Market — *FinTech · Web3 · 2020 → Now*

A digital-asset wallet and trading platform built on blockchain rails. Wallet creation, balance management, exclusive cross-platform discounts, and a remarkably stable trading experience.

`Flutter` `Web3` `NestJS` `PostgreSQL`

🔗 **[msq.market](https://msq.market)** · Play Store · App Store

---

### 🎧 PodcastDB — *SaaS · 2023 → Now*

A podcast intelligence database for PR, marketing, and advertising teams. Aggregates **2.6M+ podcasts**, with advanced search filters, audience-size estimates, ad-cost estimates, and exportable contact lists. Tiered pricing from free → enterprise.

`Next.js` `NestJS` `GraphQL` `PostgreSQL`

🔗 **[podcastdb.io](https://podcastdb.io)**

---

### 💸 Earned Wage Access — *FinTech · 2023*

Lets employees draw a portion of their earned salary before payday — reducing borrowing pressure and easing employer admin around salary advances.

`Flutter` `BloC` `Node.js`

---

### 🌙 Sakun — Sleep Stories & Noise — *Wellness · 2023*

A sleep & relaxation app for adults and children — soundscapes, fantasy bedtime stories, and interactive 3D adventures. Custom soundscapes that help users fall asleep faster.

`Flutter` `Firebase` `Audio Processing`

---

### 🤖 Quit Must / Quit Must Pro — *AI · 2021–23*

A Flutter app paired with a native Android service that periodically samples the screen and runs an **on-device NSFW classifier**. When triggered, it locks the device for a cooldown period.

`Flutter` `Java` `Android Native` `On-device ML`
